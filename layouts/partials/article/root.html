{{/* Entry point for recursive article rendering system. Delegates to optimized nested renderer for performance and maintainability. Accepts either a Page (list view), or a dict {page, single} where single=true marks standalone / single-view rendering (the top article links to its own full permalink instead of a parent #fragment). */}} {{- $page := . -}} {{- $single := false -}} {{- if reflect.IsMap . -}} {{- $page = .page -}} {{- $single = .single | default false -}} {{- end -}} {{- partial "article/nested" (dict "page" $page "single" $single) -}}
